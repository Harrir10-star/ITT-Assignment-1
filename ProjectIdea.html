Project Idea

To be perfectly honest I didn’t do this course to get a specific role. I’m actually reasonably happy with the current field I’m in at the moment. Risk and Compliance professionals are in reasonably high demand, particularly off the back of the recent Financial Services Royal Commission.

So, the role that I have chosen is more of a hypothetical, enabling me to complete a little research to determine what I need to do to become a Security Operations Centre Manager.

I like dealing with Events and issues, tracking of threat actors and their new tactics. Oh, and it also provides the opportunity to relocate to an exotic location.

Overview Personal IT Data Aggregator

The idea is to aggregate details from a multitude of sites that a household uses. While there are sites out there that aggregate to pay bills or at least provide reminders there is nothing that is simple and meets my personal needs. Therefore, I propose to build an application that will bring together the data sets of many of the sites I need to access either to update details or keep track of the data shared.

In short the users will need to identify the sites that they will add, most likely this will require user ids and passwords (presenting a security risk). The tool will scrape the information into its data base and set it out logically for the user.

Initially I was going to leave the banks out of the equation, however upon further investigations each of the major banks has a developer portal through to APIs as part of the Open banking regulations. In addition I understand that all of the tier 2 Banks are working on their open banking offerings.

There is further scope to include, Superannuation, however I’ve decided against this initially due to the security issues and many of the legal issues associated with a breach.

Motivation

This is born from the frustration I feel when I need to locate details from a utility bill or an expense(rates, dog registration, water, electricity, gas, Citylink, myki, school fees, telephone, internet), review, update or renew my insurances (cars, caravan, house, health), renew memberships( football, basketball, gym).

Some of this is held in individual sites others are just paper based. I’m sure that this is a huge issue for many households, particularly those that are time poor or poor at organisation.
